  • 5 Tbsp. - Oatmeal (1-minute version 'cause nobody's got time to wait around)
  • 1 Tbsp. - Brown Sugar
  • 1 Pinch - Cinnamon
  • 1 diced - Bananas (or is it Banannas?
  • 1 Tbsp. - Raisins (Yupp raisins, don't hate they're "gud")
  • 1 Cup - Water(Hot) or more depending what texture you want
  1. Heat up the water (if you didn't follow my recipe list and got cold water SMH.
  2. Put your 5 tablespoon of oatmeal in a semi-large bowl (1-minute version please, the cohort's not gonna want to sit around while you wait for it to finish.
  3. Stir in the brown sugar, cinnamon and raisins.
  4. Wait for the oatmeal to absorb the water (told you to get the 1-minute version LMAO)
  5. Enjoy it! Careful it's full of fiber.
